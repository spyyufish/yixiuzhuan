# 🎵 弈修传 游戏音乐生成提示词

## 第一段：备战期音乐（3分钟）

Genre: Chinese fantasy game music, ambient
Style: Traditional Chinese instruments, guzheng (古筝), xiao (箫), soft strings
Tempo: 90-100 BPM, slow and flowing
Mood: Peaceful, mysterious, cultivation atmosphere, floating clouds, mountain temple

Structure:
- Intro: Solo guzheng playing pentatonic melody, sparse, with wind chimes
- Main: Xiao and strings enter, gentle pulse, like walking through misty mountains
- Bridge: Slightly more movement, erhu solo, emotional but calm
- Outro: Return to solo guzheng, fading away

Key: C major pentatonic (C D E G A)
Dynamics: p - mp (soft to moderately soft)
No percussion, loopable

## 第二段：战斗期音乐（3分钟）

Genre: Chinese epic battle music, action
Style: Taiko drums, suona (唢呐), erhu, orchestral strings
Tempo: 140-160 BPM, driving rhythm
Mood: Intense, heroic, martial arts, energy

Structure:
- Intro: Taiko drum roll, suona call
- Build: Strings and percussion enter, rhythm established
- Main A: Full ensemble, fast pentatonic melody
- Main B: Key change up, more intense, double-time percussion
- Breakdown: Brief pause, solo erhu, then taiko re-entry
- Finale: Full power, strong ending

Key: D minor pentatonic
Dynamics: mf - ff
Loopable

---

## 第三段：BOSS战音乐（3分钟）

Genre: Chinese dark fantasy boss battle, cinematic
Style: Deep taiko drums, dizi low register, dark strings, temple bells, gongs
Tempo: 130-150 BPM, heavy groove
Mood: Epic, threatening, overwhelming power, final confrontation

Structure:
- Intro: Deep gong hits, low strings drone
- Build: Percussion enters, tension builds, dizi eerie melody
- Main A: Full dark orchestra, aggressive taiko, boss theme
- Main B: Key change, faster, "boss phase change" feel
- Climax: Everything at max intensity
- Resolution: Ominous sustained note

Key: A minor with chromatic tension
Dynamics: mp - fff
Loopable
